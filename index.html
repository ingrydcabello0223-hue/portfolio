About me

Dream big,
work with heart.

I'm Ingryd Cabello — 16, female, Filipino, and a devoted member of the Church of Christ. I live in Brgy. Simpatuyo Sitio Palagao, Sta. Teresita, Cagayan. As a Grade 11 student at Sta. Teresita National High School, I chase academic excellence (top of class), join Dancesport competitions, represent Bagwis Dance Troupe (BDT), and compete in Likhawitan, Sayawit, and other cultural events. I'm a visual artist (sketches, painting) and a multi-instrumentalist (guitar, piano, ukulele, bass). I completed the ATP program at Lyceum of Aparri (18 sessions: PowerPoint, Word, Excel, C++ logic, web foundations, HTML/CSS portfolio). Dentistry, art, and music are my future — I live to make my parents proud.
